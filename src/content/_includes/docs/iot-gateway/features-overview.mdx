The ThingsBoard IoT Gateway provides the following features:

- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/mqtt'>MQTT connector</DocLink> to control, configure, and collect data from IoT devices connected to external MQTT brokers using existing protocols.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/opc-ua'>OPC-UA connector</DocLink> to collect data from IoT devices connected to OPC-UA servers.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/modbus'>Modbus connector</DocLink> to collect data from IoT devices connected through Modbus protocol.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/ble'>BLE connector</DocLink> to collect data from IoT devices connected using Bluetooth Low Energy.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/request'>Request connector</DocLink> to collect data from IoT devices that have HTTP(S) API endpoints.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/can'>CAN connector</DocLink> to collect data from IoT devices connected through CAN protocol.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/bacnet'>BACnet connector</DocLink> to collect data from IoT devices connected through BACnet protocol.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/odbc'>ODBC connector</DocLink> to collect data from ODBC databases.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/rest'>REST connector</DocLink> to create endpoints and collect data from incoming HTTP requests.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/snmp'>SNMP connector</DocLink> to collect data from SNMP managers.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/ftp'>FTP connector</DocLink> to collect data from FTP servers.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/socket'>Socket connector</DocLink> to collect data from IoT devices connected through TCP/UDP protocol.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/xmpp'>XMPP connector</DocLink> to collect data from IoT devices connected through XMPP protocol.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/ocpp'>OCPP connector</DocLink> to communicate between Charge Point and Central System.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='config/knx'>KNX connector</DocLink> to collect data from IoT devices connected through KNX protocol.
- <DocLink product={props.product} path='custom'>Custom connector</DocLink> to collect data from IoT devices connected by other protocols. You can implement your own connector for any required protocol.
- **Persistence** of collected data to guarantee data delivery in case of network or hardware failures.
- **Automatic reconnect** to the ThingsBoard cluster.
- Flexible **mapping** of incoming data and messages to a unified format.
