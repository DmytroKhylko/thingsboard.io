---
title: Multi-Tenancy & Hierarchy
description: Learn about tenants and customers
---

Defines who owns what and how users access the system.

- **[Tenant](/docs/user-guide/ui/tenants/):** Top-level entity representing a company or business unit. Owns all resources and defines the primary security boundary.
- **[Customer](/docs/user-guide/ui/customers/):** Logical sub-organization used to isolate resources (devices, dashboards) for specific end-users.
- **[User](/docs/user-guide/ui/users/):** Authenticated account with [RBAC](/docs/pe/user-guide/rbac/) permissions. Can belong to either a Tenant or a Customer.

:::tip
Use **Customers** to securely delegate access. A Customer User can only interact with entities specifically assigned to their Customer account.
:::
