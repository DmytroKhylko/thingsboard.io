In this step, you will activate the Customer User account.  
Use the activation link copied in the [previous step](http://localhost:4324/docs/tutorial/0-introduction/4/#step-41-create-a-customer-and-a-customer-user) to complete account setup and sign in to ThingsBoard as a Customer User.

<Steps>
  1. Paste the **activation link** in a new browser tab and press **Enter**.

  2. Create a password by entering it twice, then click **Create Password**.
</Steps>

After activation, the customer user can:
- view assigned devices and their telemetry data on dashboards;
- monitor alarms related to assigned devices;
- acknowledge and clear alarms.
